About Us

Here at team InfoDump, we’ve noticed lots of polarized, biased, and misinformed opinions regarding the tech industry, particularly in the areas of AI and automation. Rather than searching the world wide web for science and technology journals and articles, we’d like to give users access to information on the ethics and implications of AI and automation in one practical location. This fully-realized product creates a convenient environment to educate oneself on the subject of AI.
